Cool Mountain Transport Safety Commitment

At Cool Mountain Transport safety comes first. Safety means customers’ freight is delivered undamaged and each driving associate goes home to their family uninjured. Professional Cool Mountain Transport drivers are committed to safe driving practices, protecting other drivers, arriving home safely, improving themselves, preserving company equipment, and ensuring that vehicles are safe before delivering to customers.

Safety initiatives include:

  • Collision-avoidance technology installed on the fleet from leading technology providers Meritor/Wabco and Bendix, collision cameras, lane departure camera, side radars, and drowsy driving warnings.
  • Tire pressure warning system, with temperature readings.
  • The newest fleet around, with the most updated technology to keep drivers safe.

Our commitment to safety starts with all of us working as a team to keep improving!
